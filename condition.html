Conditions

Brand new : The item has never been used or worn, much like brand new and should still have the tags on the garment.

Very good : The item has been used or worn and has minor visible signs of wear.

Good : The item has been used or worn and shows signs of wear.

Moderate : The item has evident signs of wear.

Please note: if a seller misrepresent the condition of their item, the buyer will be eligible for a refund and return.
